## 语法
### 链接
- `[[note#title^block|name]]`：链接到笔记note的标题title的block块处，并命名为name
- `[name](link)`：链接到外部链接link，并命名为name
### Callout
```
> [!tag] title
> text
> text
```
常用callout：
> [!example] 

> [!note] Note

> [!theorem] 

> [!definition] 

> [!proof] 

> [!caution]
